How We Work

From Quarry to Your Doorstep

A meticulous journey from the heart of Indian quarries to your space — quality inspected at every single step.

01
Inquiry & Quotation

Submit an RFQ with dimensions, quantity and destination. We respond within 48 hours with a detailed quote and high-resolution slab photographs tailored to your specifications.

02
Sample Approval

We ship physical samples (10×10 cm) directly to your door. Once you approve the stone, texture and finish, we confirm your order and commence production.

03
Production & QC

Slabs are precision-cut, polished and inspected to your exact specifications. 100% quality check with photographs sent at every production milestone.

04
Packing & Freight

Each slab is wooden-crated, foam-cushioned and loaded in sea-worthy containers. Complete export documentation and insurance provided.

05
Delivery

FOB, CIF or door-to-door delivery options. Real-time shipment tracking shared with you. Dedicated post-delivery support from our team.
